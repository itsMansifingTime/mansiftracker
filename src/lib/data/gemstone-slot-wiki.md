# Gemstone Slot (wiki snapshot)

Gemstone Slots appear on items that are upgradable with [Gemstones](https://hypixel-skyblock.fandom.com/wiki/Gemstone). One Gemstone can be applied to each Gemstone Slot. A stat bonus will be granted according to the Gemstone applied.

## Slot Types

Gemstone Slot types determine what types of Gemstones can be applied. The Stat Bonuses given by a Gemstone Slot are not determined by the slot type, but the Gemstone applied to it.

## Applying and Removing

### Applying Gemstones

Gemstones can be applied to selected items at the gemstone grinder located near [Geo](https://hypixel-skyblock.fandom.com/wiki/Geo). The Cost to Unlock (if any) must be paid in order to unlock the slot and apply the gemstone. The apply process is otherwise free of charge.

### Removing Gemstones

The applied gemstone can each be separately removed from any items at the gemstone grinder located near [Geo](https://hypixel-skyblock.fandom.com/wiki/Geo). There is a confirmation menu to prevent accidentally removing them.

| Type | Cost to remove |
| --- | --- |
| Rough | 1 [coin](https://hypixel-skyblock.fandom.com/wiki/Coins) |
| Flawed | 100 [coins](https://hypixel-skyblock.fandom.com/wiki/Coins) |
| Fine | 10,000 [coins](https://hypixel-skyblock.fandom.com/wiki/Coins) |
